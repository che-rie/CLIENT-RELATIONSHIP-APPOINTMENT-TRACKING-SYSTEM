# CLIENT-RELATIONSHIP-APPOINTMENT-TRACKING-SYSTEM
Client Relationship & Appointment Tracking System

This project involved developing a client management and appointment tracking system to support operational coordination and customer relationship management for Nye’s Lounge. The system was designed to monitor appointment statuses, follow-up requirements, customer communication, and service coordination activities.

The spreadsheet-based workflow enabled organized client tracking, operational visibility, and efficient management of customer appointments and aftercare follow-up processes.


Tools Used
• Google Sheets
• Google Workspace
• Data Validation Dropdowns
• Conditional Formatting


Key Tasks Performed
• Tracked client appointments and booking status
• Managed follow-up actions and reminders
• Organized customer service records
• Created operational workflow tracking columns
• Implemented status categorization systems


Findings
• Structured tracking improves operational organization
• Follow-up management enhances customer retention
• Appointment visibility supports workflow efficiency
• Categorized statuses simplify operational monitoring


Recommendations
• Integrate automated reminder systems
• Add customer satisfaction tracking metrics
• Implement centralized CRM functionality
